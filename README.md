Generative artificial intelligence, exemplified by GPT-4, is reshaping the marketing landscape by enabling scalable, personalized, and adaptive content generation, thereby significantly improving both marketing efficiency and effectiveness.

This study is conducted in response to Expleo’s proposed research topic and adopts a structured analytical approach to progressively define the research objectives and system development goals. The analysis covers industry trends over the past year, current market dynamics, and competitor positioning, from which actionable insights are derived.
Building upon this analysis, I developed a lightweight content generation tool based on OpenAI’s latest large language model (LLM) API. The tool is characterized by the following features:

1.	Lightweight, fast, and efficient
2.	Capable of generating and summarizing the top ten most popular customized content formats from the previous year
3.	Strictly aligned with Expleo’s three core strategic objectives: brand positioning, talent acquisition, and potential client engagement
4.	Each content format is explicitly mapped to and closely aligned with these three strategic objectives
5.	For each content format, the tool clearly specifies its intended purpose, target audience, content delivery strategy, and priority, and supports local content storage to assist marketing professionals in their workflows
6.	Based on the automated content generation capability, an AI-driven green cycle framework for marketing promotion and analytics automation is further conceptualized for reference
The study concludes with validation results, sample outputs, and strategic recommendations for AI-driven precision marketing.

In addition, a draft report (non-academic submission version) detailing the design framework and conceptual approach of the tool is provided in the associated GitHub repository for reference and reproducibility. 以GPT-4为代表的生成式人工智能正在重塑营销领域，借助可规模化、个性化及自适应的内容生成能力，显著提升了营销效率与效果。

本文围绕Expleo提出的研究主题，采用结构化分析方法，逐步明确了研究目标与系统开发目标。研究内容覆盖过去一年的行业趋势、当前市场格局及竞争对手定位，并从中提炼出具有可执行价值的洞察。

基于以上分析，我开发了一款基于OpenAI最新大型语言模型（LLM）API的轻量级内容生成工具。该工具具备以下特点：
1.	轻便、快捷、高效
2.	可生成并总结去年十大最受欢迎的定制化内容形式
3.	严格对应 Expleo 的三大核心战略目标：品牌定位、人才吸引及潜在客户吸引
4.	每种内容形式均与上述三大战略目标紧密对齐
5.	针对各类内容形式，工具会明确说明其应用目的、目标受众、内容交付策略、优先级，并提供本地保存等功能，以协助市场营销人员开展工作
6.	基于自动内容生成功能，进一步构想了一套市场营销推广分析自动化的 AI 绿色循环图，供参考使用

研究最后通过验证结果、示例输出以及对 AI 驱动精准营销的战略建议进行总结。
